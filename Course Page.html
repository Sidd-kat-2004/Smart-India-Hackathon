Introduction to the Indian Constitution

Welcome to the course on the Indian Constitution. This course will provide you with an understanding of the key principles of the Constitution, its history, and how it shapes governance in India.

The Preamble of the Indian Constitution sets out the ideals of justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity. It reflects the vision of the founding fathers and serves as a guide to the spirit of the Constitution.

Throughout this course, we will explore various sections, amendments, and fundamental rights granted by the Constitution.
